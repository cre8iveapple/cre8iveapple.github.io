Instructions

The aim of the game is to guess which character out of 24 possibilities their opponent has selected.

The computer has randomly assigned you a character.

Take it in turns to ask yes or no questions, such as Do you have black hair? or Are you wearing glasses?.

There are some question ideas below

Clicking on a character or question will eliminate them from the board.

The winner is the first person who correctly guesses their opponents character.

Refreashing the page will change your Random character and reset your board.
